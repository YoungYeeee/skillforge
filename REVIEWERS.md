# Reviewers

SkillForge reviewers are domain experts who volunteer to evaluate skill modules in their areas of expertise.

**Want to join?** Open an issue using the [Apply to be a Reviewer](https://github.com/yourusername/skillforge/issues/new?template=reviewer-application.md) template.

## Reviewer Responsibilities

- Review assigned modules within **14 days** of being tagged
- Use the [review checklist](./.github/PULL_REQUEST_TEMPLATE/skill-review.md)
- Give specific, actionable feedback — not vague approval or rejection
- Decline promptly if you don't have capacity (so we can invite someone else)

There is no minimum commitment. Review when you can, decline when you can't.

---

## Recognition

Reviewers are credited:
- In the module they reviewed (`Reviewed by: @username — credential`)
- In this registry
- In release notes when a reviewed module ships

---

## Inactive Reviewers

Reviewers who haven't responded to 3 consecutive review requests over 6 months will be moved to an "Inactive" list. They can reactivate anytime by commenting on an issue.
