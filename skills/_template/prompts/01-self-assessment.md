# Prompt: Self-Assessment — [Skill Name]

**Purpose:** Establish your baseline before starting. This helps the AI calibrate its teaching to your actual level.

**When to use:** Day 1, before any other prompts in this module.

**Expected duration:** 20–30 minutes

---

## The Prompt

```
I want to assess my current level in [skill name] so I can learn it more effectively.

Please conduct a structured self-assessment with me. Do NOT lecture me or teach me yet — just assess.

Here's how I'd like you to do it:

1. Ask me one question at a time. Wait for my answer before asking the next.
2. Start with a broad question, then probe deeper based on my responses.
3. After 6–8 questions, give me an honest assessment of:
   - What I clearly understand
   - Where my understanding has gaps or misconceptions
   - What level I'm at (novice / developing / proficient)
   - Where I should focus first

Start with this question: "Tell me about a recent situation where you tried to apply [skill name]. What happened?"

If I haven't applied it before, ask: "Tell me what you think [skill name] means in your own words, and give me an example of what it looks like in practice."

Remember: your job right now is to understand where I am, not to teach me.
```

---

## After the Session

Write down (don't skip this):

1. **What the AI identified as my strengths:**

2. **What the AI identified as my gaps:**

3. **My starting level (novice / developing / proficient):**

4. **One thing that surprised me about my own understanding:**

---

## Notes for Learners

- Be honest. An inflated self-assessment will cause the AI to pitch learning at the wrong level.
- If the AI starts teaching before completing the assessment, remind it: "Please finish the assessment first."
- Save this assessment. You'll compare it to your exit assessment when you graduate.
